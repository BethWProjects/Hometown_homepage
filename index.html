Visit Crested Butte

Enjoy skiing, biking and fishing in this quaint town in southwest Colorado.

Top three activities to do in Crested Butte, CO

Take some turns on the mountain

Enjoy challenging terrain on the North Face, or take it easy on the Red Lady Chair.

Explore on the water

Paddleboard the many lakes or glide the easy flowing Slate River.

Snowshoe the backcountry

Bring your favorite pup and enjoy the view snowshoing the many trails in the area.
